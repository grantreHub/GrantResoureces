Grant resources

Service

Grant Writing and Development

We are proud to deliver turn-key grant services including identification and analysis of grant opportunities, extensive research, conducting needs assessments, program model development, creation of management plans, evaluation design, budget planning, completion of required federal forms, and electronic submission by a team of highly qualified grant professionals and expert consultants. We embrace the team approach and will work collaboratively with you to develop a program model that meets the strategic goals of your organization and aligns with the funders' priorities.

Grant Management

We provide a range of services to help you effectively manage grant programs, ensuring the highest standards of accountability. Our priority, when your grant is funded, is to support you in implementing a quality program that produces results. As your ongoing partner, we take on the frustrating details of grants management to help you keep your program on track.

Capital Campaign

We work with you to assess and provide guidance for your new or existing Startups or Nonprofits. Our staff will work with your Startup or Nonprofit to develop a strategic plan for the business's launch, management, and growth.

Nonprofit Filling and Startup

Business and Nonprofit strategic plan and development
Marketing and financial practices
Business and Nonprofit management and expansion

Coaching and Mentoring

We provide training and tools to help raise capital through the collective effort of friends, family, customers, and supporters. This approach is based on the collective efforts of a large pool of individuals and resources—and leverages their networks for greater reach and exposure.

Training and Workshops

We provide ongoing training and workshop to equip and strengthen Startups and Nonprofits: Grant Writing and Development, Financial Management, Fundraising, Capital Campaign, Marketing, and Nonprofit Legal Practices.
