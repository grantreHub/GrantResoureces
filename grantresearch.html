Grant Research and Writing

We understand how important grant funding is to your Nonprofit organization. Whether your organization is new to the grant writing process or looking to improve an existing grant writing process, our team will help meet or exceed your funding goals through effective planning, research and grant writing services.

Grant Research and Writing Services

Our streamlined process will help your organization identify grant opportunities and then prepare award winning grant applications:

01
Grant Readiness Assessment

In this discovery phase, we start by understanding your grant funding requirements. We then conduct an assessment to make sure you have all the necessary components in place to increase your chances of success for grant applications (e.g. detailed program design and budgets, research supported with quantitative and qualitative data, etc.).

02
Grant Research

It can be an arduous task to research various grant databases to identify opportunities that truly fit your funding needs. Our full time grant experts search through various search engines, databases and grant repositories to prepare a summary report of grant opportunities with a good fit to your program and organization. The summary report includes research and fit analysis for each grant, such as alignment to the Grantmakers mission and funding priorities, past grant awards, etc..


03
Grant Writing

Our experienced grant writers can assist your organization by preparing tailored grant applications to meet the grant application requirements. Our team offers a comprehensive approach from start to finish to make sure grant applications are timely submitted with compelling narratives, budgets and more. We offer one time grant writing support and monthly packages based on your needs and budget constraints.

04
