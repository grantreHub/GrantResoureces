How to Start a Not-for-Profit

Do you have a vision for your community? Do you have a desire to serve others? Would you like to make a living helping others? Do you have a solution to a problem? Have you ever dreamed of becoming a not-for-profit organization but just don't know the steps to take? If you answered yes to any of these questions you should participate in this webinar "How to Start a Not-for-Profit.".


You will learn

  • The essential steps to starting a not-for-profit and getting grant ready.
  • The difference between a for-profit and not-for-profit corporation.
  • What to file, when to file and where to file.
  • The benefits and responsibilities of a 501c3 (tax exempt status).
  • How to prepare your incorporation documents properly when seeking federal and state tax exemption.
  • The difference between a public charity and a foundation.

BONUS WEBINAR: How to Obtain Funding

It may appear that passion drives success, but the reality is there is a lot of strategy involved in any successful endeavor. Sometimes we "luck up" but we do not want to depend on luck. Luck is not sustainable. In this webinar we will share with you a strategy for successfully getting your organization funded quickly. If you are one of these for people, this workshop is for you.
